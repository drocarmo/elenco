Elenco

Google Extension

Elenco serves as a to do extension app for the Chrome browser. Opening a new tab activates Elenco, displaying current tasks.

Syncs Everywhere

It syncs tasks to all the Chrome browsers your google account is logged into. Edit & delete tasks and it syncs eveywhere.

“Inbox Zero”

Elenco was built for people who enjoy an ‘inbox zero’ feel at the end of the day. A task list built into your browser looks good when it's empty.

Open Source

Elenco was built out in the open. We're building new features and killing issues as they come. It's whatever you want it to be.
